## Collection Themes

### Elemental Groupings

**Fire Signs** (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius)
- Bold, passionate, radiant
- Warm color palettes (reds, golds, oranges)
- Dynamic energy and presence

**Earth Signs** (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn)
- Grounded, stable, practical
- Earth tones and greens
- Solid, enduring presence

**Air Signs** (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius)
- Intellectual, communicative, visionary
- Light blues, yellows, violets
- Ethereal, flowing energy

**Water Signs** (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces)
- Emotional, intuitive, mystical
- Blues, silvers, purples
- Depth and fluidity
